How Do We Work

Education

Charity through education is a transformative force that empowers individuals and communities, breaking the cycle of poverty and opening doors to a brighter future. It recognizes that education is not just a privilege but a fundamental right that every child deserves. By investing in education, charities create opportunities for marginalized and underprivileged individuals to acquire knowledge, skills, and confidence, unlocking their true potential.

Clothing

Charity through clothing is a meaningful way to address the basic needs and dignity of individuals facing challenging circumstances. It recognizes that clothing is not just about practicality but also about self-expression, comfort, and a sense of belonging. Through clothing donations and initiatives, charities strive to provide warmth, protection, and confidence to those who are less fortunate.

learn more

Food

Charity through food is a vital response to the pressing issue of hunger and food insecurity in our communities. It recognizes that access to nutritious food is a fundamental right that should be afforded to all individuals, regardless of their socioeconomic status. Through food banks, community kitchens, and meal distribution programs, charitable organizations strive to alleviate hunger, nourish bodies, and provide a lifeline for those in need.

About World Vision India

World Vision India is a child-focused humanitarian organisation that has been serving the most vulnerable children and their families, for over 70 years. The non-profit organisation passionately serves in 200 districts, impacting around 26 lakh children and their families in over 6200 communities, spread across 24 states and 1 union territory. With over seven decades of experience at the grassroots, we employ proven, effective development, public engagement and relief practices empowering vulnerable children and communities living in contexts of poverty and injustice to become self-sufficient and bring lasting change.

With you, we can build a nation fit for children. Join us. Together for children. For change. For life.
